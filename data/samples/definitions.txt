"Adjusted EBITDA" means, in relation to a Measurement Period, operating profit 
for that period before taxation:
(a) before deducting any Interest Expense;
(b) before taking into account any Depreciation and Amortization; and
(c) before taking into account any Extraordinary Restructuring Costs, provided 
    that the aggregate amount of such costs shall not exceed 10% of 
    Adjusted EBITDA for that Measurement Period.

"Total Net Debt" means, at any time, the aggregate amount of all obligations 
for Borrowings (including Bonds and Bank Loans) less the aggregate amount of 
Cash and Cash Equivalents held by the Group.
